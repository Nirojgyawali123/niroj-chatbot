Who I Am

I'm Niroj Gyawali, a first-semester BSc CSIT student with a deep passion for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. My journey into tech started with curiosity — wondering how machines can learn, think, and solve real-world problems.

Beyond academics, I'm actively involved in building and nurturing a tech community in Lumbini. As an organizer of Lumbini Tech Month, I help connect students and professionals with learning opportunities in various IT fields.
